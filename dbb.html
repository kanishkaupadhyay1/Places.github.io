ABOUT DUBAI BEACH



Dubai, a city known for its opulence
and architectural marvels, also boasts some
of the world's most stunning beaches. Jumeirah
Beach, stretching along the city's coastline, is
an iconic destination. With its pristine white sands
and the backdrop of the striking Burj Al Arab, it's a
symbol of Dubai's luxury.



One of the beach in Dubai is Jumeira Beach that is a
white sand beach that is located and named after the Jumeirah
district of Dubai, United Arab Emirates, on the coast of the
Persian Gulf.
